### Derivation of the Slip Evolution Equation

We start from the **traceless spatial Einstein equation** (ij component with i ≠ j) in Newtonian gauge.

For a theory with non-minimal coupling `F(φ)R`, the perturbed Einstein equations receive contributions from both the metric variation and the variation of `F(φ)`.
